About Us

We are a full service linen company providing Wash and Fold, Pickup and Delivery, Linen Rental Service and Equipment Sales and Repair. Our main focus is in the commercial sector that services health and wellness spas, gyms and fitness clubs, as well as the restaurant industry and medical field.

We also offer programs for employee laundry that allows you to have your staff's work attire professionally cleaned at the same time your other products are. It's a great headache free program that your employees will appreciate.

Give us a call and we will customize your program today!

Our equipment service and sales division will get you up and running in no time when you have that unexpected breakdown. We can send our technicians out right away to assess the situation, and even pick up your linen products to wash so you don't get behind on your schedule. If you prefer maintaining your own on-site laundry we also offer a full range of the top name companies to replace your old worn-out equipment.
