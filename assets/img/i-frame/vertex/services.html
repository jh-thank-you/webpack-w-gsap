Services

Linen Rentals
(Life just got a whole lot easier)

Like the idea of providing top quality linens for your customers but don't want to pay for the inventory? Our rental service will take care of the headache for you. We offer a rental program that's all inclusive when you rent the product from us. We will pick up wash and re-deliver based on your schedule, all you pay is one low rate!

We can customize your order based on the quality of product you're looking for, and we even offer custom designs if you want your company logo on the product as well. What better way to show your customers that you truly care about the quality of their experience. Give our sales department a call and we will set up your custom program today!
